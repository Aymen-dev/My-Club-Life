My Club Life

Welcome to My Club Life, the ultimate web app for diving into the club experience of your choice! Our platform provides an all-in-one solution for club related topics such as member recruitment, participating in events and overall club performance.
With My Club Life, you can easily discover clubs in your university, rate and review them if you are already a member, and participate with your club in yearly planned competitions. Our evaluation system ensures the discovery of the true levels of clubs comparing to each other in the social and student life, considering criteria like club size, activities and events.
